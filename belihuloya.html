Belihuloya

About This Place

A scenic river valley in the Ratnapura district where the Belihul Oya river flows through lush forest and rocky gorges, creating natural pools perfect for swimming and bathing. The area is a popular weekend getaway for those seeking a refreshing outdoor escape, with the surrounding hills offering pleasant walks through tea estates and jungle.

Quick Tips

  • Check river conditions before swimming especially after heavy rain
  • Weekends can be busy with local visitors so weekdays are quieter
  • Several small guesthouses in the area offer comfortable overnight stays
  • The surrounding hills are good for short walks and nature exploration
  • Bring a change of clothes and a towel for the river swimming
