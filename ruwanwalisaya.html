Ruwanwelisaya

About This Place

A massive white dagoba built in the 2nd century BC, considered one of the most sacred Buddhist monuments in Sri Lanka. Its enormous dome and the surrounding terrace draw pilgrims and visitors who walk the perimeter in quiet reflection.

Quick Tips

  • Walking on the marble terrace barefoot can get hot midday so go early or late
  • Dress modestly
  • The site is best appreciated slowly so allow time to walk around
  • Combine with other Anuradhapura ruins nearby
  • Photography is welcomed in most areas
