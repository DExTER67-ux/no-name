Hikkaduwa Marine National Park

About This Place

A protected coral reef just offshore from Hikkaduwa beach, home to a diverse marine ecosystem including sea turtles, tropical fish, and coral formations accessible by snorkelling or glass bottom boat. The shallow reef makes it one of the most accessible marine wildlife experiences in Sri Lanka, suitable for all ages and swimming abilities.

Quick Tips

  • Snorkelling gear can be rented from shops along the beach
  • Glass bottom boat tours are available for non swimmers
  • Never touch or stand on the coral as it causes serious damage
  • Sea turtles are often seen near the reef so keep a respectful distance
  • Morning visits offer the calmest water and best visibility
