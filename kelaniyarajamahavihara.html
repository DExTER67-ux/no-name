Kelaniya Raja Maha Vihara

About This Place

A historic Buddhist temple believed to have been visited by the Buddha himself, featuring vividly painted murals depicting Buddhist stories and Sri Lankan history throughout the temple halls. It remains an active and important place of worship close to Colombo.

Best Time To Visit

Year round, with the annual Duruthu Perahera festival in January being a particularly vibrant time to visit.

Quick Tips

  • Dress modestly and remove shoes before entering
  • The murals are best viewed slowly so allow time inside
  • The temple is easily reached as a half day trip from Colombo
  • Respect ongoing prayers and ceremonies
  • Photography is generally allowed but check locally
