Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi

About This Place

One of the oldest documented trees in the world, grown from a cutting of the original Bodhi tree under which the Buddha attained enlightenment. It has been continuously tended for over 2,000 years and remains an active site of pilgrimage and worship.

Quick Tips

  • Dress modestly and remove shoes near the inner enclosure
  • Photography may be restricted in certain areas so check signage
  • Visit alongside other Anuradhapura sacred sites
  • The tree is fenced and protected so view it from the designated areas
  • Respect ongoing worship taking place
