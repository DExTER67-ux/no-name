About This Place

A quieter stretch of beach just north of Trincomalee town, with calm clear water and a relaxed atmosphere. Less visited than Nilaveli further up the coast, it offers a more local feel with small guesthouses and seafood restaurants lining the shore

Quick Tips

  • Calmer and less crowded than Nilaveli
  • Good base for visiting Trincomalee harbour and hot springs
  • Seafood restaurants along the beach are affordable and fresh
  • Morning swims are best before the heat builds
  • Combine with a day trip to Pigeon Island
