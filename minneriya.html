Minneriya National Park

About This Place

Home to one of the most spectacular wildlife events in Asia, known as The Gathering, where hundreds of wild elephants congregate around the Minneriya reservoir between July and October as the dry season causes the water level to drop and fresh grass to grow on the exposed lake bed. This annual event is one of the largest gatherings of Asian elephants in the world.

Quick Tips

  • Visit between July and October for the best chance of seeing The Gathering
  • Afternoon safaris are best as elephants gather near the reservoir later in the day
  • Combine with nearby Kaudulla National Park for a full day of wildlife
  • Book a licensed jeep in advance during peak gathering season
  • Bring a zoom lens as elephants may be spread across the far side of the reservoir
