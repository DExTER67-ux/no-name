Kuda Oya Falls

About This Place

A peaceful waterfall set within the forests of the Monaragala district, away from the busy tourist circuits of the hill country. The falls offer a calm and natural setting for those who enjoy spending time in undisturbed forest environments, with the surrounding area home to interesting wildlife and birdlife.

Quick Tips

  • Very few tourists visit so expect a genuinely quiet experience
  • A local guide is recommended to find the falls easily
  • Wear sturdy footwear suitable for forest trails
  • Bring insect repellent and enough water for the visit
  • Combine with other Monaragala area natural attractions
