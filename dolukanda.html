Dolukanda

About This Place

A prominent rocky hill rising above the town of Kurunegala, with an ancient Buddhist temple at its summit that has been a place of worship for centuries. The climb offers views over the town's famous Elephant Rock and the surrounding lowlands.

Quick Tips

  • The path to the summit follows stone steps for most of the way
  • Remove shoes before entering the temple at the top
  • Early morning visits are peaceful before day visitors arrive
  • Respectful dress is required
  • The view over Kurunegala town and Elephant Rock is the highlight
