About This Place

A laid back beach town famous for whale watching boat trips and a long stretch of golden sand backed by coconut palms. The bay is also a popular surf spot for beginners, and the small rocky outcrop just offshore is a favourite sunset photo spot.

Quick Tips

  • Book whale watching tours in advance during peak months
  • Bring seasickness tablets if you're prone to it
  • The main beach gets busy in the evening
  • Try the rooftop cafes for sunset views
  • Respect Marine life
