Abhayagiri

About This Place

A vast monastic complex that was once home to thousands of monks and one of the most important centres of Buddhist learning in ancient Asia. The site includes a large stupa, ancient bathing pools, and extensive ruins spread across a forested area.

Highlights

Ancient Monastery

Best Time To Visit

December to March for comfortable walking conditions across the spread out site.

Quick Tips

  • The site is large so a bicycle or tuk tuk helps cover the distance between ruins
  • The Kuttam Pokuna twin ponds are a highlight not to miss
  • Bring water and sun protection
  • Allow at least half a day to explore properly
  • Combine with other Anuradhapura sites in the same trip
