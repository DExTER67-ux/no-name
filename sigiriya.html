Sigiriya Rock

About This Place

An ancient rock fortress rising 200 metres above the surrounding plain, built by King Kasyapa in the 5th century. Visitors climb through landscaped water gardens, past famous frescoes of celestial maidens, and through the lion's paw gateway to reach the palace ruins at the summit. It is one of the most iconic and visited sites in Sri Lanka.

Best Time To Visit

December to April for the best weather, though it can be visited year round. Early morning is best to avoid heat and crowds.

Quick Tips

  • Arrive before 7am to beat the crowds; wear comfortable shoes with grip
  • Bring water and a hat
  • The frescoes are at mid level so don't rush past them
  • Hire a guide at the entrance for the full historical context
