Bopath Ella

About This Place

A wide and distinctive waterfall in the Ratnapura district, named for its shape which resembles a Bo leaf when viewed from below. The falls cascade 30 metres into a large pool that is popular for swimming, and the surrounding area has a lively atmosphere with local visitors and food stalls at weekends.

Best Time To Visit

Year round due to the high rainfall in Ratnapura, though weekdays are quieter and more relaxed for swimming.

Quick Tips

  • The pool at the base is popular for swimming, check conditions before entering
  • Weekends can be very busy with local visitors so weekdays are calmer
  • Food stalls near the falls sell local snacks and drinks
  • The Bo leaf shape of the falls is best seen from a distance
  • Wear footwear you do not mind getting wet near the pool area
