Mihintale

About This Place

Considered the birthplace of Buddhism in Sri Lanka, this sacred hill is where Buddhism was first introduced to the island over 2,000 years ago. A long stairway leads up through several terraces of ancient ruins to a stupa at the summit with panoramic views.

Best Time To Visit

Early morning to avoid the heat of the long stairway climb, especially around the Poson Poya festival in June.

Quick Tips

  • The climb involves over 1,800 steps so pace yourself
  • Bring water for the ascent
  • Visit during Poson Poya for a unique cultural experience if timing allows
  • Comfortable footwear is essential
  • The summit view is worth the effort
