Pigeon Island National Park

About This Place

A small island marine park just off the coast of Nilaveli on the east coast, home to one of the best preserved coral reefs in Sri Lanka. The crystal clear waters around the island are teeming with blacktip reef sharks, sea turtles, and colourful tropical fish, making it one of the top snorkelling destinations in the country.

Best Time To Visit

April to September during the east coast dry season when the sea is calmest and visibility underwater is at its clearest.

Quick Tips

  • Boat trips to the island depart from Nilaveli beach nearby
  • Snorkelling gear can be rented from operators on the mainland
  • Do not touch or stand on the coral reef as it causes lasting damage
  • Blacktip reef sharks are harmless but maintain a calm and respectful distance
  • Go early in the morning for the calmest water and fewest visitors
