About This Place

A long stretch of pristine white sand on the east coast with calm, clear turquoise water that makes it ideal for swimming. It's also the main departure point for boat trips to Pigeon Island for snorkelling.

Highlights

Snorkelling

Whale & Dolphin watching

Peaceful, uncrowded atmosphere

Best Time To Visit

April to September, when the east coast enjoys its dry season opposite to the rest of the island.

Quick Tips

  • Combine a beach day with a boat trip to Pigeon Island
  • The beach is much quieter than west coast equivalents
  • Check sea conditions before swimming during monsoon transition months
  • Nearby guesthouses offer good value compared to busier areas
