Kirindi Ella Falls

About This Place

A beautiful waterfall tucked away in the Ratnapura district, flowing through dense rainforest in one of the wettest regions of Sri Lanka. The falls are relatively unknown to most tourists, making it a rewarding destination for those who enjoy exploring quieter and more natural spots away from the main tourist trail.

Best Time To Visit

Year round due to the high rainfall in the Ratnapura region, though the falls are most powerful during and just after the monsoon season.

Quick Tips

  • Arrange a local guide as the area is not well signposted
  • Wear waterproof footwear as trails can be muddy
  • Bring insect repellent for the forested paths
  • Combine with other Ratnapura area attractions
  • Carry enough water and snacks as facilities are very limited nearby
