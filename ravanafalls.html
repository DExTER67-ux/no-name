Ravana Falls

About This Place

A wide, multi-tiered waterfall just outside Ella town, easily visible and accessible from the main road. Named after the legendary king Ravana from the Ramayana epic, it is one of the most photographed waterfalls in Sri Lanka due to its convenient location.

Best Time To Visit

Quick Tips

  • Easily visited as a quick stop while exploring Ella
  • Swimming is possible in the lower pools but check conditions first
  • Very accessible as the falls are right beside the road
  • Early morning visits avoid the bus tour crowds
  • Combine with other Ella attractions on the same day
