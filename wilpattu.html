Wilpattu National Park

About This Place

Sri Lanka's largest national park, famous for its unique natural lakes called villus which are shallow sand rimmed basins filled with rainwater that attract large concentrations of wildlife. The park is one of the best places in the country to spot leopards and sloth bears in a less crowded environment compared to Yala, giving it a more remote and wild atmosphere.

Quick Tips

  • Much less crowded than Yala so a good choice for a quieter safari experience
  • Book a licensed jeep and guide at the park entrance
  • Early morning safaris give the best chance of leopard and sloth bear sightings
  • The park is large so full day safaris cover more ground than half day trips
  • Bring plenty of water and snacks as facilities inside the park are very limited
