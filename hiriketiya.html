About This Place

A small horseshoe shaped bay that has become popular with surfers and yoga retreat visitors in recent years. The compact beach is surrounded by cliffs and palm trees, giving it a more secluded feel compared to larger beaches nearby

Quick Tips

  • The bay is small so it can feel crowded at peak times;
  • Great spot for beginner surfers due to gentle waves
  • Several cafes serve healthy food options nearby
  • Sunset views from the surrounding cliffs are lovely
