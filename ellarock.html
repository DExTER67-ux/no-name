Ella Rock

About This Place

A more challenging hike than Little Adam's Peak, Ella Rock rewards those who make the effort with spectacular views from a higher vantage point over the Ella Gap valley. The route passes through jungle and tea estates and takes around two to three hours return.

Best Time To Visit

January to April for the clearest views, though the trail can be done year round with care.

Quick Tips

  • The trail can be confusing so consider hiring a local guide
  • Wear hiking shoes with good grip
  • Bring enough water for two to three hours
  • The summit can be windy so bring a light layer
  • Start early to allow enough time to return before dark
