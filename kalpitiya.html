About This Place

A remote peninsula on the northwest coast known for kitesurfing and dolphin watching trips. The lagoon side offers calm shallow water perfect for beginners, while the ocean side has stronger winds favoured by more experienced kitesurfers.

Plan Your Visit

Quick Tips

  • Book kitesurfing lessons in advance during peak wind season
  • Dolphin watching tours run early morning for best sightings
  • The area is quite remote so plan transport ahead
  • Bring sun protection as shade is limited
  • Lagoon side is calmer for non-kitesurfing activities.
