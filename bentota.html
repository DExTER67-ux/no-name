About This Place

A wide sandy beach known for water sports including jet skiing, banana boating, and windsurfing on the nearby river lagoon. It's a popular spot for both day trips from Colombo and longer stays at the beachfront resorts.

Best Time To Visit

Quick Tips

  • Negotiate prices for water sports activities before booking
  • The river lagoon is good for calmer activities like kayaking
  • Turtle hatcheries nearby are worth a visit
  • Lifeguards are present on the main beach areas
  • Book water sports early in the day before it gets busy
