Ramboda Falls

About This Place

One of the most accessible and impressive waterfalls in the hill country, dropping 109 metres in a long elegant cascade visible from the main Kandy to Nuwara Eliya road. The falls sit within lush tea estate country and a small viewing platform and hotel nearby make it an easy stop for travellers on this popular route.

Best Time To Visit

Year round, with the strongest flow during the monsoon months. The surrounding tea estates look particularly green and lush after rainfall.

Quick Tips

  • Easily visible and accessible from the main Kandy to Nuwara Eliya road
  • A small hotel near the falls has a good viewing point from its terrace
  • Combine with a tea factory visit in the surrounding estate area
  • The falls can be viewed from the road without any hiking required
  • Morning light gives the best conditions for photography
