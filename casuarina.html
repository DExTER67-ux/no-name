About This Place

A shallow, calm beach on the Jaffna peninsula named after the casuarina trees lining the shore. The gentle slope into the water makes it one of the safest beaches in the country for swimming, popular with local families

Quick Tips

  • Very shallow water extends far out, good for children
  • Limited facilities so bring your own supplies
  • Quieter on weekdays compared to weekends
  • Combine with other Jaffna peninsula sightseeing
  • Shade from casuarina trees is useful during midday
