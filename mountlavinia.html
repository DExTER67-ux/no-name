About This Place

A wide urban beach close to Colombo, popular for evening walks, street food, and watching the sunset over the Indian Ocean. The colonial era Mount Lavinia Hotel sits on the headland overlooking the bay.

Quick Tips

  • Visit in the late afternoon for food stalls and sunset views
  • Swimming conditions vary so check locally before going in
  • The train station is right next to the beach making it easy to reach from Colombo
  • Weekends tend to be busier with local visitors
  • Try grilled corn and other street snacks along the promenade.
