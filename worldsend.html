World's End

About This Place

A dramatic sheer cliff drop at the edge of the Horton Plains plateau, with a near vertical fall of around 870 metres to the lowlands below. On clear days the view extends all the way to the southern coast. It is accessed via a scenic flat walk across the plateau from the park entrance.

Best Time To Visit

December to April for clear mornings before the mist rolls in. Arrive at the park as early as possible.

Quick Tips

  • Enter the park before 7am to reach World's End before cloud obscures the view
  • The walk to World's End from the entrance is about 9km return
  • Baker's Falls is on the same route so combine both
  • Wear layers as the plateau is cold in the morning
  • Bring water and snacks
