Manawela Falls

About This Place

A lesser known waterfall near Kandy, flowing through forest and farmland in a quieter part of the Central Province. It offers a more local and undiscovered experience compared to the more famous falls elsewhere in the hill country, making it a good choice for visitors who want to explore beyond the usual tourist routes.

Quick Tips

  • Very few tourists visit so expect a quiet and local experience
  • A tuk tuk from Kandy is the easiest way to reach the area
  • Wear footwear suitable for uneven forest paths
  • Combine with other Kandy area attractions for a full day out
  • Bring your own supplies as there are no shops or facilities nearby
