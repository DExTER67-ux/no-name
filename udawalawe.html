Udawalawe National Park

About This Place

The best park in Sri Lanka for guaranteed elephant sightings, with large herds roaming freely across open grasslands surrounding the Udawalawe reservoir. The park is also home to water buffalo, crocodiles, sambar deer, and a wide variety of waterbirds, making it an excellent destination for both wildlife enthusiasts and first time safari visitors.

Best Time To Visit

Year round as elephants are almost always visible, though May to September and December to March offer the most comfortable weather for safaris.

Quick Tips

  • Elephant sightings are almost guaranteed making it great for families
  • Morning safaris offer the best light for photography
  • Book a licensed jeep at the park entrance or in advance online
  • Visit the Elephant Transit Home nearby to see rescued baby elephants
  • Bring sunscreen and a hat as the open grasslands offer little shade
