Alakola Ella

About This Place

A hidden waterfall near Kandy, tucked within lush forest and farmland in the Central Province. It is one of the lesser visited falls in the region, offering a quiet and unspoilt natural experience for those who take the time to seek it out away from the more popular tourist spots.

Best Time To Visit

Year round, with the strongest flow during and after the monsoon season from May to September.

Quick Tips

  • Ask locals for directions as the falls are not well signposted
  • A tuk tuk from Kandy is the most convenient way to reach the area
  • Wear footwear suitable for uneven and potentially muddy paths
  • Bring water and snacks as there are no facilities nearby
  • Combine with other Kandy area day trips for a full itinerary
