Galle Fort

About This Place

A well preserved colonial era fortress built originally by the Portuguese and later expanded by the Dutch, now home to a charming old town filled with boutique shops, cafes, and historic buildings along cobbled streets. Walking the ramparts at sunset is one of the most popular activities in the south of the island.

Highlights

Best Time To Visit

Year round, with late afternoon being ideal for walking the fort walls at sunset.

Quick Tips

  • Walk the entire rampart for the best views over the ocean and old town
  • Explore the small streets inside the fort for boutique shopping and cafes
  • The lighthouse area is a popular sunset spot
  • Wear comfortable shoes for the cobbled streets
  • Visit the maritime museum for local history
