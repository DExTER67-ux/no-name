Riverston

About This Place

A stunning mountain peak in the Matale district sitting at the edge of the Knuckles mountain range, offering some of the most dramatic and wide ranging views in Sri Lanka. On a clear day the panorama stretches from the central highlands all the way to the coast, and the area is increasingly popular with hikers and photographers seeking an alternative to the busier viewpoints in the country.

Quick Tips

  • Visit early morning for the clearest views before cloud builds up
  • The road to Riverston can be rough so a sturdy vehicle is recommended
  • Bring warm layers as it can be cold and windy at the top
  • Combine with a visit to the nearby Knuckles range for a full day
  • Go on weekdays for a quieter experience as it is growing in popularity
