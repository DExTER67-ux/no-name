About This Place

A long, quieter stretch of coastline known for its dramatic surf, rock pools, and laid back guesthouses scattered along the shore. Less developed than beaches further west, it's a good base for exploring nearby lagoons and turtle nesting sites.

Quick Tips

  • Some sections have strong currents so check before swimming;
  • Visit nearby turtle hatcheries in the evening
  • Rock pools near Hummanaya blowhole are worth exploring
  • Accommodation is more spread out so transport helps
  • Quieter than other south coast beaches, good for relaxation
