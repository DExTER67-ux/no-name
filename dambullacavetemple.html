Dambulla Cave Temple

About This Place

A complex of five cave temples carved into a massive rock outcrop, containing over 150 Buddha statues and extensive ceiling paintings dating back more than 2,000 years. It is the largest and best preserved cave temple complex in Sri Lanka.

Quick Tips

  • Remove shoes before entering each cave
  • Dress modestly
  • The climb to the caves involves stairs so wear comfortable footwear
  • Watch for monkeys near the entrance who may approach for food
  • Combine with a visit to nearby Sigiriya
