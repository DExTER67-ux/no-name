Batalegala

About This Place

Also known as Bible Rock's neighbouring peak, Batalegala is a forested hill in the Kegalle district offering a quieter hiking experience away from the more commonly visited spots in the hill country. The summit gives views over the surrounding rubber and tea estates.

Quick Tips

  • Very few tourists visit so local guidance is helpful
  • The trail passes through forest so insect repellent is recommended
  • Bring enough water for two to three hours
  • Good for those wanting a less crowded hiking experience
  • Rubber estate paths can be slippery when wet
