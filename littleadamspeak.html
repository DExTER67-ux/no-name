Little Adam's Peak

About This Place

A short and accessible hike just outside Ella town offering sweeping views over the surrounding valley, tea plantations, and the Nine Arches Bridge below. Much easier than the real Adam's Peak, it's suitable for most fitness levels and takes around 45 minutes to reach the top.

Quick Tips

  • Start early morning before cloud rolls in
  • The path passes through tea estates so stay on marked trails
  • Comfortable trainers are sufficient
  • Several viewpoints along the way are worth stopping at
  • Bring a camera for the Nine Arches Bridge view below
