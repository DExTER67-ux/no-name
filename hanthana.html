Hanthana Mountain Range

About This Place

A range of hills just outside Kandy city, popular for day hikes with views back over the city and surrounding tea estates. The trails are accessible from the city making it a convenient option for those based in Kandy who want a half day outdoor experience.

Best Time To Visit

December to April for the clearest views, though the range can be visited year round.

Quick Tips

  • Several trailheads are accessible by tuk tuk from Kandy
  • Start early to avoid afternoon heat
  • The trails vary in difficulty so check your route before starting
  • Bring water and snacks
  • The view back over Kandy lake is worth finding
