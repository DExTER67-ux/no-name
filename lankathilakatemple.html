Lankatilaka Temple

About This Place

A striking rock temple built into a granite outcrop, combining Buddhist and Hindu architectural elements in a single structure. Its elevated position offers panoramic views over the surrounding paddy fields and hills, making it as scenic as it is historically significant.

Quick Tips

  • Combine with nearby Embekke Devalaya as they are close together
  • Remove shoes before entering the temple
  • The surrounding paddy field views are best from the temple steps
  • Dress modestly
  • Quieter than major temples making for a peaceful visit
