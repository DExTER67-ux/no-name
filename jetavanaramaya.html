Jetavanaramaya

About This Place

Once one of the tallest structures in the ancient world, this massive brick stupa stands as a testament to the engineering skill of ancient Sri Lankan civilisation. Though partially restored, its sheer scale remains awe inspiring against the surrounding ruins.

Quick Tips

  • The site covers a large area so allow time to explore the surrounding ruins
  • Bring water as shade is limited
  • Combine with nearby Abhayagiri on the same visit
  • A bicycle is a great way to get around the wider Anuradhapura sacred city
  • Respect the religious significance of the site
