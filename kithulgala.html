Kitulgala

About This Place

A lush riverside town in the Kegalle district, best known as Sri Lanka's premier white water rafting destination on the Kelani River. The area is also famous as the filming location for the classic film Bridge on the River Kwai, and the surrounding rainforest offers excellent birdwatching and trekking opportunities alongside the river adventures.

Best Time To Visit

May to December when the river flow is strongest and the rafting experience is most exciting. The dry season from January to April offers calmer conditions suitable for beginners.

Quick Tips

  • Book rafting tours in advance as operators fill up quickly during peak season
  • Wear clothes you do not mind getting wet and bring a change of clothes
  • The surrounding forest is excellent for birdwatching so bring binoculars
  • Several guesthouses along the river offer overnight stays
  • Canyoning and abseiling are also available as additional activities
