Diyaluma Falls

About This Place

The second highest waterfall in Sri Lanka at 220 metres, cascading down in a single dramatic drop visible from the main road below. At the top, a series of natural infinity pools offer a unique swimming spot with panoramic views over the valley.

Quick Tips

  • The hike to the top pools takes around an hour and can be slippery
  • Swim only in designated calmer pools and avoid the edge near the drop
  • Hire a local guide for the safest route up
  • Bring water shoes for the rocky terrain
  • The base view from the road is also impressive without the climb
