Bomburu Ella

About This Place

A wide and powerful waterfall in the Nuwara Eliya area, known for its impressive breadth rather than just its height. The falls cascade over a broad rocky face surrounded by misty hill country scenery, creating a dramatic and photogenic setting that is particularly striking after heavy rainfall.

Best Time To Visit

October to January for the fullest flow, though the surrounding hill country scenery is beautiful throughout the year.

Quick Tips

  • Best visited in the morning before mist reduces visibility
  • The viewing area can be slippery so wear shoes with good grip
  • Combine with other Nuwara Eliya waterfalls on the same day
  • Bring a rain jacket as the area receives frequent showers
  • The falls look most impressive from a distance so find a wider viewpoint
