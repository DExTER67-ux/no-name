Kaudulla National Park

About This Place

A relatively new national park connected to Minneriya by a wildlife corridor, allowing elephants to move freely between both parks depending on water and food availability. The park centres around the Kaudulla reservoir and is an excellent alternative to Minneriya when elephant herds have migrated north, offering equally impressive sightings with fewer visitors.

Quick Tips

  • Check with local guides whether elephants are currently in Kaudulla or Minneriya
  • Less visited than Minneriya so often offers a quieter safari experience
  • Afternoon safaris are best as elephants emerge near the reservoir later in the day
  • Combine with Minneriya for a full elephant safari day trip
  • Bring binoculars and a zoom camera lens for the best views across the reservoir
