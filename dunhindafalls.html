Dunhinda Falls

About This Place

A picturesque waterfall reached via a pleasant 30 minute forest walk from the main road, cascading 64 metres into a misty pool below surrounded by lush jungle. It is one of the most accessible and popular falls in the hill country.

Highlights

Forest Walk

Waterfall Views

Easy Access

Best Time To Visit

Year round, though the falls are fullest during and after the monsoon season.

Quick Tips

  • The walk to the falls is mostly flat and suitable for most fitness levels
  • Vendors along the path sell snacks and drinks
  • The viewing platform can get crowded on weekends
  • Swimming is not advised in the pool below due to strong currents
  • Bring a light rain jacket as mist from the falls can reach the path
