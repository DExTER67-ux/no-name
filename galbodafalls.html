Galboda Falls

About This Place

A charming waterfall accessible via a short train ride and walk from Galboda station on the scenic hill country railway line, making the journey itself part of the experience. The falls drop through a peaceful forested gorge and the approach on foot from the station passes through beautiful rural hill country scenery.

Best Time To Visit

Year round, with the most dramatic flow after heavy rainfall. The train journey itself is worth doing in any season.

Quick Tips

  • Take the train from Kandy or Nuwara Eliya and alight at Galboda station
  • The walk from the station to the falls is short and pleasant
  • Check train times in advance as services are limited
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes for the path from the station
  • Combine with the wider scenic hill country train journey for a memorable day
