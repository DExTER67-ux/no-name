Pahanthudawa Falls

About This Place

A scenic waterfall located in the Ratnapura district, flowing through the lush wet zone forest that the region is known for. The falls are surrounded by rich biodiversity including many bird species, making it a pleasant destination for nature lovers and those interested in birdwatching alongside the waterfall scenery.

Best Time To Visit

Year round due to the consistently high rainfall in the Ratnapura area, though the wet season brings the most impressive water flow.

Quick Tips

  • Bring binoculars if interested in birdwatching along the forest trail
  • Wear waterproof footwear as the paths can be wet and muddy
  • A local guide is helpful for navigating the forest trails
  • Insect repellent is recommended for the forested approach
  • Carry your own water and snacks as facilities are limited
