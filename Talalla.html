About This Place

A long, undeveloped stretch of golden sand backed by palm trees, with a much quieter atmosphere than nearby tourist beaches. It's a good choice for those wanting an uncrowded beach experience with simple guesthouse accommodation.

Quick Tips

  • December to March for calm seas and dry weather.
  • Check for currents before swimming as it can be unpatrolled
  • Good base for cycling to nearby villages
  • Limited dining options so plan accordingly
  • Lovely for long beach walks at sunrise or sunset
