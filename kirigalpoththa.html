Kirigalpoththa

About This Place

The second highest mountain in Sri Lanka at 2,395 metres, located within Horton Plains National Park. The summit can be reached via a trekking route that branches off the main Horton Plains trail, passing through montane cloud forest and open grassland.

Quick Tips

  • A permit and guide are required for the Kirigalpoththa trail
  • The hike takes around four to five hours return
  • Start early from the park entrance; wear waterproof hiking boots
  • This is a more serious hike than the main Horton Plains circuit
