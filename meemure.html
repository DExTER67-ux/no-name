Meemure

About This Place

One of the most remote and isolated villages in Sri Lanka, nestled deep within the Knuckles mountain range in the Kandy district. The journey to Meemure itself is part of the adventure, passing through spectacular mountain scenery, river crossings, and dense forest. The village retains a traditional way of life largely unchanged by modern tourism.

Best Time To Visit

January to April when the access road is drier and easier to navigate, as the route can become very difficult during heavy rain.

Quick Tips

  • A four wheel drive vehicle is strongly recommended for the access road
  • Plan to stay overnight as the journey takes several hours each way
  • Respect the local community and ask before photographing villagers
  • Bring all necessary supplies as shops in the village are very limited
  • A local guide from Kandy can arrange the visit and accommodation
