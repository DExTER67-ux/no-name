Sera Ella Falls

About This Place

A scenic waterfall in the Matale district, flowing through forested terrain in the central hill country. The falls are set in a peaceful natural environment and are popular with local visitors for picnics and relaxing by the water, offering a quieter alternative to the more well known falls in the area.

Quick Tips

  • Popular with local families so weekends can be lively
  • Wear footwear suitable for rocky and wet surfaces near the falls
  • Bring a picnic as the surrounding area is pleasant for relaxing
  • Combine with a visit to the Dambulla area nearby
  • Carry your own water as facilities on site are minimal
